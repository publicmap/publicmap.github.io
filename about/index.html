publicmap.github.io

Map are one of the earliest scientific tools of humans and remains one of the most educative tools to help understand our relationship with the land. Public map is a tool to support that cause.

This map is built by the public

  • Since 2004, over 6 million people have formed a community to collectively create an Open Street Map of earth.
  • Since 2001, over 93 million people have helped collect the sum of human knowledge.
